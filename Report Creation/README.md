## üß≠ Overview Page

This page delivers a high-level snapshot of overall sales performance.

### Key Insights:
- **Core KPIs**  
  Revenue, total opportunities, success rate, and average sales cycle days offer a quick health check of the pipeline.

- **Revenue by Quarter and Month**  
  Identifies peak and slow periods in sales activity.

- **Opportunity Distribution**  
  Visualizes the breakdown of opportunities across stages:
  - Prospecting  
  - Engaging  
  - Won  
  - Lost  

- **Top 5 Products and Accounts**  
  Highlights the most profitable products and highest-value clients.

- **Regional Sales Distribution**  
  Shows how performance varies across regions, helping uncover growth opportunities.

## üì¶ Products Page

This page focuses on understanding product-level performance.

### Key Insights:
- **Top Products**  
  Identifies:
  - Top selling products  
  - Most won deals  
  - Highest success rates  
  - Fastest selling products  

- **Revenue by Series and Product**  
  Compares performance across product families and individual items.

- **Success Rate vs Series Average**  
  Highlights products outperforming their series benchmark.

- **Sales Cycle Days vs Series Average**  
  Identifies products with faster deal closure.

- **Price Difference Analysis**  
  Shows variance between suggested retail price and actual sales price, helping assess pricing strategy and competitiveness.

## üåç Market Reach Page

This page evaluates performance across markets, sectors, and geographies.

### Key Insights:
- **Market Coverage Overview**  
  Displays total:
  - Accounts  
  - Sectors  
  - Countries  
  Along with the top 3 in each category by revenue.

- **Revenue and Success Rate by Sector**  
  Identifies the most profitable industries and those with the highest deal-closing efficiency.

- **Sales Cycle Duration by Sector**  
  Reveals which sectors close deals faster or slower.

- **Account-Level Drill-Down**  
  Includes a link to a detailed account page showing:
  - Revenue by client  
  - Revenue by client country  
  - Success rate  
  - Sales cycle duration  
  - Top purchased products  

This helps identify key clients and upsell or cross-sell opportunities.
